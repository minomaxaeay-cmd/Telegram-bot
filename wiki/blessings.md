# Blessings

Blessings are temporary, hard-bounded buffs bought with [Faith](currencies.md) ✝️
(generated by the [Chapel](ref_buildings/chapel.md)). They are the **Faith sink** in the
[closed economy](economy.md): Faith is spent and destroyed when a blessing is bought.

## The three blessings
| Blessing | Faith | Duration | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Blessing of Zeal ⚔️ | 50 | 1h | **+10% combat damage** (attacking & defending) |
| Blessing of Plenty 🌾 | 40 | 1h | **+25% [Grain](currencies.md) production** |
| Blessing of the Ward 🛡️ | 60 | 1h | **−10% incoming damage while defending** |

(`config.BLESSING_DEFS`.)

## Rules
- **One per effect.** Only one blessing of each effect can be active; buying again
  refreshes its timer.
- **Temporary.** Each lasts `duration` seconds (1h) then expires automatically.
- **Bounded.** Magnitudes are small and capped — Zeal equals the
  [Highlander](ref_tribes/highlander.md) tribe's +11% combat (a temporary, comparable buff), which [MG5](../balance_overhaul/MG5_sampled_non_domination.txt)
  already cleared — so blessings cannot break non-domination.

## How effects apply
- **Zeal / Ward** feed directly into [combat](combat.md): the bot reads the attacker's
  and defender's active multipliers (`get_blessing_combat_mult` / `get_blessing_defense_mult`)
  and passes them into `simulate_battle` (`atk_combat_mult`, `def_combat_mult`,
  `def_ward_keep`). With no blessing active the multipliers are `1.0` (no change).
- **Plenty** multiplies [Farm](ref_buildings/farm.md) grain output in the income tick.

## Where to buy
The ⚔️ Army menu → **✝️ Blessings** (`blessings:menu` → `bless_buy:<key>`).
